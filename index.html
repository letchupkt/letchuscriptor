LetchuScriptor

by Letchu Pkt

Convert DuckyScript scripts to Keyboard scripts that you can use with various platforms.

Why DuckyScript and why Keyboard? DuckyScript is simple and easy to learn and has become a standard in the BadUSB and pentesting community. Keyboard library is a standard library for Arduino which makes it compatible with various Arduino boards including Digispark and ESP32.
